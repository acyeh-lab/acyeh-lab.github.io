About Us

Welcome to the Albert Yeh Lab, where we are dedicated to understanding the biology of stem cell transplantation.

Our Research

Our lab focuses on understanding T-cell responses, microbiome interactions, and the mechanisms driving graft-versus-host disease (GVHD). We utilize a combination of murine models and computational approaches to study the adaptive immune system.
