About Us

Welcome to the Albert Yeh Lab, where we are dedicated to exploring innovative solutions in immunology and oncology. Our mission is to translate cutting-edge science into meaningful advancements for patient care.

Our Research

Our lab focuses on understanding T-cell responses, microbiome interactions, and the mechanisms driving post-transplant complications like graft-versus-host disease (GVHD). We combine murine models with computational immunology to uncover novel therapeutic targets.

Meet the Team

Our diverse team of researchers and collaborators bring together expertise in molecular biology, computational modeling, and clinical sciences.
